# Funding & backers — honest assessment

**Last updated:** 2026-07-07

## Do you need backers?

| Goal | Job + nights/weekends | Backers help? |
|------|------------------------|---------------|
| **Music app** (Lane A — sovereign locker player) | Painful but you proved ~6 weeks to serious scope | Optional — accelerates QA, design, release |
| **OS 0.1** (Linux shell + server sync + one social primitive) | Hard alone with full-time job | **Yes, likely** — 6–18 months focused |
| **Full OS + marketplace + vault + vote** | Unrealistic solo with job | **Yes** — or community + grants + very long horizon |

**Backers are not required to keep ideating** or to ship Music updates. They become important when **calendar time** matters more than **typing speed** — e.g. job pulls you away for 40h/week.

## Would anyone back this?

**Some people would — not “most VCs.”** The pitch that wins:

> “We already built a multi-platform sovereign music stack in weeks. Next: the OS layer — your server, your locker, your stations — freedom over extraction. Music is the wedge; marketplace and federation follow.”

### Who might say yes

| Source | Fit | Why |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Privacy / sovereignty angels** | High | Personal server, no surveillance, EU privacy mood |
| **FOSS foundations & grants** | Medium | NLnet, Sovereign Tech Fund, Prototype Fund — if open-source and documented |
| **Community / crowdfunding** | Medium | Niche users who want self-hosted music + future OS |
| **Family & friends** | Variable | Belief in you, not the TAM slide |
| **Strategic indie** | Medium | Someone who wants federated commerce / music, not another ad network |

### Who usually says no (at OS stage)

| Source | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Classic VC** | No 100M-user path yet; “OS” sounds like 10 years; regulated money/vote scares them |
| **Big tech** | Conflicts with platform lock-in |
| **Banks** | Opposite of “without banks” narrative |

## What backers need to see

1. **Working product** — Music app on real devices (you have this).  
2. **Clear wedge** — Lane A: “best sovereign locker player” before “replace iOS.”  
3. **Narrow next milestone** — e.g. “OS 0.1 in 12 months: identity + server sync + social DM,” not full marketplace.  
4. **Open docs** — `sandbox-os` chronicle + public roadmap (this repo).  
5. **Team or sustainability plan** — solo + job = “bus factor”; backers want honesty about time.

## Paths without traditional backers

| Path | Tradeoff |
|------|----------|
| **Keep job, ship Music, defer OS** | Slow OS calendar; no dilution |
| **Part-time → save runway → 6-month sprint** | Self-funded “backer” |
| **Open-source + donations** (GitHub Sponsors, etc.) | Small but aligned users |
| **Grants** | Application time; must fit funder mission |
| **Co-op / foundation** | Users own the project; slow governance |

## Recommendation for your situation

1. **Don’t raise for “the whole OS” yet** — raise (or grant-apply) for **one funded milestone**: e.g. *Sandbox OS 0.1 + Music as default station*.  
2. **Use Music as proof** — demos, F-Droid, short video of locker + server + vinyl.  
3. **Document in chronicle** — backers read progress; chat history is not due diligence.  
4. **Job is not failure** — many sovereignty projects started nights/weekends (Linux, Mastodon early days). Backers fund **momentum and clarity**, not only raw hours.
